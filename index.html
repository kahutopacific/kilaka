What is Know Before You Dig (KBYD)?
KBYD is Fiji's first pre-dig safety initiative, championed by Kahuto Pacific. In Australia and New Zealand, checking for underground utilities before digging is mandatory. Kahuto is bringing that same standard to Fiji to prevent costly and dangerous utility strikes.
What is Ground Penetrating Radar (GPR)?
GPR sends electromagnetic pulses into the ground. When these pulses hit buried objects — pipes, cables, concrete — they bounce back and create an image of what's underground. Kahuto uses the Leica DS2000, a dual-frequency GPR that detects both metallic and non-metallic utilities in a single pass.
Why should I survey before I dig?
A single utility strike can cost FJD 20,000 – 150,000+ in emergency repairs, downtime, liability claims, and fines. Worse, it can cause electrocution, flooding, service disruption, and worker injuries. Prevention costs up to 70× less than a strike.
What is AS 5488?
AS 5488 is the Australian Standard for subsurface utility information. It defines quality levels from QL-D (desktop study) to QL-A (physical verification). Kahuto holds both parts in-house: AS 5488.1:2022 and AS 5488.2:2022. We deliver QL-B as standard — the highest non-invasive level.
What aerial survey services does Kahuto offer?
Kahuto operates a DJI M300 RTK drone equipped with Zenmuse L2 LiDAR and a Zenmuse P1 (45MP) camera. We deliver topographic surveys, volumetric analysis, corridor mapping, flood modelling, forestry analysis, and high-resolution orthophotos.
What is the Moana Data Service?
Moana Data Service is an ADB-backed geospatial data platform hosted on Koordinates. It provides Pacific Island nations with accessible, high-quality geospatial data for climate resilience, infrastructure planning, and disaster risk management.
Where does Kahuto operate?
Kahuto Pacific operates in Fiji and Solomon Islands. Our team includes DBYD-certified utility locators, PhD-qualified GPR specialists, and experienced field surveyors. Call 944 3226 or email survey@kahutopacific.com.
KBYD kaa cheez hai?
KBYD matlab "Mo Kila Ni O Sebera Ni Keli Keli" — zameen khodne se pehle pata karo ki andar kaa hai. Fiji me abhi koi system nahi hai underground pipe aur cable ke baare me jaane ke liye. Kahuto Pacific ii kaam kar raha hai — GPR machine se zameen ke andar sab kuch detect kare hai, bina khode. Ek baar utility pe strike lage to FJD 20,000 se 150,000+ tak ka nuksan ho sake hai.
Na cava na KBYD?
Na KBYD se "Mo Kila Ni O Sebera Ni Keli Keli" e dua na porokaramu me qarauni na veika sa biu e loma ni qele — na wai pipe, na waqa ni livaliva, kei na cables — ni bera ni keli. E vakarautaki taki mai na Kahuto Pacific me baleta na nodra savasava kei na nodra maroroi na lewe ni vanua.
