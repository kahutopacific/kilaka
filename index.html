FAQ
What is Know Before You Dig (KBYD)?
KBYD is Fiji's first pre-dig safety initiative, championed by Kahuto Pacific. In Australia and New Zealand, checking for underground utilities before digging is mandatory. Kahuto is bringing that same standard to Fiji.
What is GPR?
GPR sends electromagnetic pulses into the ground. When these pulses hit buried objects, they bounce back and create an image. Kahuto uses the Leica DS2000, a dual-frequency GPR that detects both metallic and non-metallic utilities.
Why survey before digging?
A single utility strike can cost FJD 20,000 - 150,000+. Prevention costs up to 70x less than a strike.
What is AS 5488?
AS 5488 is the Australian Standard for subsurface utility information. Kahuto holds both parts: AS 5488.1:2022 and AS 5488.2:2022. We deliver QL-B as standard.
What aerial services does Kahuto offer?
DJI M300 RTK with Zenmuse L2 LiDAR and P1 camera. Topographic surveys, volumetric analysis, corridor mapping, flood modelling, and orthophotos.
KBYD kaa cheez hai?
KBYD matlab zameen khodne se pehle pata karo ki andar kaa hai. Kahuto Pacific GPR machine se zameen ke andar sab kuch detect kare hai, bina khode.
Na cava na KBYD?
Na KBYD se "Mo Kila Ni O Sebera Ni Keli Keli" e dua na porokaramu me qarauni na veika sa biu e loma ni qele ni bera ni keli.
